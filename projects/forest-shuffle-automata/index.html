Forest Shuffle Automata

Taken from here.

Scoring app.

▼ Set-up
  • Randomly remove 30 cards (55 with Alpine, 90 with Alpine + Woodland).
  • Shuffle 1 winter card into 10 cards.
  • Shuffle the remaining cards and place on top of the pile with the winter card.
  • Draw 6 cards.
  • Place 5 cards into the clearing.
  • You take the first turn, at the end of each turn click "Next" to draw an automata card, resolve it and then it is your turn again.
  • Always close gaps in the clearing, without changing their order.
  • The game ends immediately when the winter card is drawn.
  • Lindens now score 3 points if you have at least 3 Lindens in your forest.
  • The Great Spotted Woodpecker now scores 10 points if you have at least 10 trees in your forest.
